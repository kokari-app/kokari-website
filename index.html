Kokari is a new app for collecting, experiencing, and sharing digital treasures.

Whether you want to showcase your NFTs to your friends or send an interactive bouquet of flowers to your loved one, Kokari lets you share immersive experiences that are unique, personal, and magical.

And for 3D artists, Kokari offers a revolutionary platform for imagining, creating, and sharing their work directly with their audience.
